About me

Hi, my name is Tanvi Gavali. I am a Software Engineer based in New York, United States. I hold a Bachelor of Science (Hons) in Software Engineering from the University of Central Lancashire, United Kingdom and currently studying Masters in Computer Science program in the Pace University - New York.

I have one year of industrial experience working as a Systems Developer at Medication Management Robotics, a pharmaceutical automation company based in the UK. I have full-stack experience creating back end Java software to process systems data and manage databases, front-end desktop and web solutions for customers, and interfacing with various scanning and printing hardware for on-premises solutions.

I am passionate about computer science in all forms and have multiple hobbies relating to software development, including participating in game jams and working on personal projects on my GitHub.
